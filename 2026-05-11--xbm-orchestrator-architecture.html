Én scheduled orchestrator: capture først, research etter verifisert completion — ikke tidsbasert 08:10-hack.

Level 1 — Architecture

  • One cron entry starts one orchestrator.
  • Capture writes local files and state.
  • Completion gate verifies files before research.
  • Research creates local Markdown + action board.

Level 2 — Contracts

  • xbm.capture.completed internal event object.
  • Research keyed by source_run for idempotency.
  • Run-log mapping is source of truth for item numbers.
  • Partial captures are allowed, but must be explicit.

Safety boundaries

  • X/browser stays read-only.
  • GBrain lookup is read-only in research phase.
  • No GitHub publish or push.
  • No GBrain promotion without a separate explicit command.
